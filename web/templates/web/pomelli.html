Pomelli AI Review: Create On-Brand Ads Without the Creative Grind

Pomelli AI transforms your website into a smart brand identity and instantly generates consistent, ready-to-use marketing content. It helps businesses create social media posts, ads, and visuals in seconds while maintaining brand tone and style.

Open Pomelli on Google Labs ↗

What is Pomelli AI?

Pomelli AI is a digital assistant from Google Labs that helps you generate on-brand marketing content. It studies your site to build a Brand DNA profile, then uses that to keep copy and visuals consistent across posts, ads, and other assets.

Key features

Brand DNA analysis

Infers tone, colors, and style from your site so generated content matches your brand instead of generic “AI slop.”

Content & visuals

Creates copy and image-style assets for social, ads, and campaigns with a multi-platform focus.

Pros and cons

Pros

  • Fast iteration for many formats
  • Brand-consistent by design
  • Beginner-friendly UI (Labs product)

Cons

  • Output can feel repetitive
  • Less control than a full design stack
  • Quality depends on your source site

Availability, sign-in, and pricing can change; see Google Labs (Pomelli).
