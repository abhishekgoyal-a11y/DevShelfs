What is HeyGen?

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform specialising in realistic talking avatar videos. You type a script, choose an AI avatar (or create one from a short recording of yourself), and HeyGen produces a polished video of that avatar speaking your script—complete with natural gestures and lip sync.

Its Video Translation feature has made it especially popular: upload an existing video and HeyGen will dub it into another language with the original speaker's cloned voice and lip movements adjusted to match.

Key features of HeyGen

1. AI avatar video creation

Choose from a library of over 100 stock avatars or create a personalised digital twin from a 2-minute video recording of yourself. Type your script and generate a video in minutes.

2. Video Translation (40+ languages)

Upload a video and HeyGen translates the speech, clones the speaker's voice, and adjusts lip movements—producing dubbed content that looks and sounds like the original presenter speaking the target language.

3. Instant Avatar (personalised digital twin)

Record two minutes of yourself speaking and HeyGen creates a photorealistic digital twin. You can generate unlimited videos in your own likeness without ever being on camera again.

4. Interactive Avatar API

Developers can embed a real-time interactive AI avatar into their products—useful for customer service bots, virtual assistants, and training interfaces that need a human face.

5. Templates and scenes

Choose from professional templates for product demos, training videos, social media content, and corporate communications—the avatar is placed in a suitable scene automatically.

6. Voice cloning

Clone a voice from a short sample and use it to narrate scripts—combined with an avatar, this produces videos that look and sound like a specific person delivering your content.

How HeyGen works

  • Select or create an avatar (stock or personal digital twin)
  • Type or paste your script—or let AI generate it
  • Choose a voice, background, and template
  • HeyGen renders the video—typically in a few minutes
  • Download or share directly from the platform

Pros and cons of HeyGen

Pros

  • Most realistic AI avatar quality available
  • Video translation with lip-sync is genuinely impressive
  • Create a personal digital twin from 2 minutes of footage
  • Large template library for quick starts
  • Interactive avatar API for product embedding

Cons

  • Pricing is per video credit—can get expensive at volume
  • Avatars still look slightly artificial on close inspection
  • Video translation quality varies by language
  • Digital twin creation requires a consent recording process

Bonus section

Tips for better HeyGen videos

  • Write for speech: short sentences and natural pauses read better on video than dense paragraphs
  • Add punctuation for pacing: commas and full stops control where the avatar pauses—use them deliberately
  • Use the translation feature early: uploading a high-quality original produces the best dubbed output
