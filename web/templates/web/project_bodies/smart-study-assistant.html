This is a small browser app built with Streamlit. You paste notes or a short reading, click one button, and an AI model turns that text into study material: a short summary, key points, and practice-style questions you can read on the same page.

Purpose: help you study faster than rereading long notes from start to finish.

Overall flow

User enters notes
      ↓
Clicks button
      ↓
App sends notes to AI
      ↓
AI generates:
  - Summary
  - Key points
  - Quiz
      ↓
Displayed on screen
